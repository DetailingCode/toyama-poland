The values shown are the maximum charging voltage (bulk/absorption phase), not the battery'"'"'s operating voltage. The charger temporarily supplies a higher voltage, which — after charging finishes and a few hours of rest — settles on its own to a lower resting voltage. Gel and AGM batteries should not be discharged below 11V, as this negatively affects their lifespan.

Applications

Where LiFePO4 excels

Campers and caravans

Up to 70% less weight than a lead-acid battery of the same capacity — less load on the axle.

Boats, sailboats, and motorboats

No memory effect and low self-discharge — safe on-board power protected by a built-in BMS.

Electric outboard motors

Low weight with high peak power — a comfortable trolling drive on boats and kayaks.

Solar and off-grid systems

3500-5000 charge cycles — a service life many times longer than classic energy storage.

Backup power

Stable operating voltage across most of the discharge cycle, without the voltage sag typical of lead-acid.

Lightweight electric vehicles

High capacity-to-weight ratio — scooters, carts, and mobile equipment requiring compact power.

Advantages

Why this series

01

Free Bluetooth app

The app lets you monitor the current available capacity, voltage, and charge/discharge current in real time.

02

LED display

A display is factory-fitted on the top of the case. It shows the current voltage, current, and battery temperature.

03

Low weight

LiFePO4 batteries are up to 70% lighter than lead-acid batteries.

04

Maintenance-free operation

LiFePO4 batteries are completely maintenance-free — no need to top up electrolyte or regularly check fluid levels.

05

Very long service life

2000+ charge/discharge cycles.

06

No memory effect

They can be topped up at any time and don't need to be charged to full.

07

Very low self-discharge

Under ideal conditions they can be stored for several months without recharging (at 0–20°C).

08

Fast charging

LiFePO4 batteries can be charged at a much higher current compared to lead-acid batteries.
